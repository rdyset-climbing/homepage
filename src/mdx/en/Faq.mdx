### FAQ

#### Is ReadySet an official dealer for these brands?

No. ReadySet has no official relationship with any of the companies listed here. All information included on this site is subject to change, and should always be verified on the manufacturer's website.

#### How can I order?

ReadySet does not support ordering holds directly. Instead, please contact each hold maker directly.

#### Can you add/remove/modify my information from this site?

If you're a Japanese climbing hold maker and would like to get in touch, please [email us](hello@rdyset.com).
